Privacy Policy

1. Who We Are

Brightside is a consulting studio based in Amman, Jordan, providing strategy, operations, design, and digital services to organizations. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information you share with us through our website at brightside-co.com.

We only collect information you voluntarily provide through our contact form. This may include your name, email address, organization name, and the message you send us. We do not collect any other personal data through this website.

We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, analytics tools, or any third-party data collection services on this site.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information you submit through our contact form is used solely to respond to your inquiry and communicate with you about potential or ongoing work. We do not use it for marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

4. How We Store Your Information

Contact form submissions are received via Formspree and delivered directly to our business email inbox. We do not maintain a separate database of contacts. Information is retained only as long as necessary to manage our business relationship with you.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties. Your information is used exclusively for the purpose of responding to your inquiry and delivering services if engaged.

6. Payments

This website does not process any payments. All financial transactions are handled directly and separately from this website. No financial data is collected or stored here.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you, to request correction or deletion of that information, or to withdraw consent for us to use it. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@brightside-co.com.

Our website may contain links to client websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their policies independently.

10. Contact
